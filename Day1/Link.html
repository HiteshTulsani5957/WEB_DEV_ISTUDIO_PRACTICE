Paragraphs are essential building blocks of writing, designed to organize thoughts and develop a single, focused idea. Each paragraph typically starts with a topic sentence that introduces the main point, followed by supporting sentences that provide details, evidence, or examples to explain that point thoroughly. Proper structure, such as indentation or skipping a line, makes text easier to read and allows the reader to follow the argument smoothly
